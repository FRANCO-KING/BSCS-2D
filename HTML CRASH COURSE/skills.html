MY SKILLS

LEADERSHIP
- Leadership is the ability to influence, motivate, and enable others to achieve a common goal. It's not just about being in charge; it's about inspiring and empowering others to reach their full potential.

PROBLEM SOLVING
- Problem solving is a cognitive process that involves identifying, analyzing, and resolving problems. It's a fundamental skill that we use every day, from simple tasks like fixing a broken appliance to complex challenges like developing new technologies.

GROWTH MINDSET
- The belief that abilities can be developed through effort and learning. This mindset fosters a willingness to embrace challenges and learn from mistakes.
